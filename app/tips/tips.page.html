Tips for a Healthy Pregnancy

Before you can take care of your new baby, you need to take care of yourself and your unborn child.
Take a prenatal vitamin Exercise regularly Write a birth plan Educate yourself Change your chores (avoid harsh or toxic cleaners, heavy lifting) Track your weight gain (normal weight gain is 25-35 pounds) Get comfortable shoes Eat folate-rich foods (lentils, asparagus, oranges, fortified cereals) SEE MORE
